PHILOSOPHY & ITS IMPACT

Explore Why Philosophy is Important

Welcome to the World of Philosophy

According to the Cambridge Dictionary, philosophy is defined as: "the use of reason in understanding such things as the nature of the real world and existence, the use and limits of knowledge, and the principles of moral judgment."

Why is Philosophy Important?

Philosophy is important because it helps people understand the world around them. Science is a provable and objective way to understand the world around us. Whereas, philosophy is a subjective way to understand the world around us. As human beings, we have an innate desire to understand the world and to understand our place in it. Belief systems such as philosophies, religions, and political ideologies are attractive to people because they provide them with a source of meaning in their lives.

How Philosophers Have Impacted Society

Philosophy has helped shape mathematics, political systems, educational programs, languages, & cultures all across the world.

Contributions to Society From Philosophers:
  • The Greek Philosopher Pythagoras Created the Pythagorean Theorem
  • The Scottish Philosopher Adam Smith Created the Idea of Free-Market Capitalism
  • The Chinese Philosopher Confucius Created the Golden Rule
  • Socrates, a Greek Philosopher, Came up With the Socratic Method
