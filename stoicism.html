Exploring the Philosophy of Stoicism

What Exactly is Stoicism?

Stoicism is a philosophy that emphasizes personal responsibility, introspection, respectfulness, honor, & mental fortitude.

The 4 stoic virtues are wisdom, temperance (self-control), justice, & courage.

In recent times, Stoicism has experienced a resurgance in popularity. Many young adults find that Stoicism helps them deal with the adversities of life, and become better versions of themselves in the process. It is amazing that Stoicism is still relevant nowadays. the philosphy was created over 2,000 years ago. Ancient Stoic wisdom truly is timeless.

Inspirational Stoic Quotes

  • "The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts." - Marcus Aurelius
  • "You become what you give your attention to." - Epictetus
  • "It never ceases to amaze me: we all love ourselves more than other people, but care more about their opinion than our own." - Marcus Aurelius

  • "Difficulties strengthen the mind, as labor does the body." - Seneca

Famous Stoic books include Marcus Aurelius' Meditations, The Discourses of Epictetus, & Seneca's On The Shortness of Life.
